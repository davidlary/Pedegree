Education & Career

Ready to advance your career? IEEE has the tools and resources to support you at every stage of your professional journey.

Discover industry advancements with IEEE Xplore®, our extensive digital library. Build your knowledge through our expert-led courses. Connect with members and network at over 2,000 events around the world. No matter where you are in your career, IEEE has the resources you need to stay at the forefront of innovation.

Guidance and Support Throughout Your Professional Journey

No matter what stage you are in your career, IEEE can help you gain experience, build a community, and shape the future of your industry.

Advance Your Career

Young Professionals

Start your career off right with IEEE. Network with leaders and peers in your field, develop your technical knowledge, and access valuable member benefits.

Students

Want to boost your resume and career? Get hands-on experience, connect with mentors, join competitions, and build leadership skills with IEEE.

Industry

Fresh insights, a supportive community of peers, and up-to-date skills are key to staying one step ahead. IEEE offers members all of that—and more.

Academic

Level up your academic career with IEEE. Our members get exclusive opportunities to gain leadership experience, collaborate with peers, and gain industry insights.

Public Service

We help technical experts stay on top of emerging trends and issues, and provide access to the opportunities and global networks they need to be successful.

Retirees

Continue to share your expertise and make an impact in your field. IEEE offers ways to stay involved and help shape the next generation of engineers.

Education and Training To Support Your Goals

Continue your education

    IEEE Learning Network

    Advance your career with the IEEE Learning Network (ILN), our online portal for continuing education on topics including artificial intelligence, autonomous vehicles, digital privacy, career development, and more. Earn professional development hours (PDHs) and continuing education units, certificates of completion, and/or digital badges.

    IEEE Learning Network

    IEEE eLearning Library

    Keep your team up to date with a subscription to the IEEE eLearning Library, which offers hundreds of hours of continuing professional education courses in current and emerging technologies as well as professional skills. Our programs are developed with leading experts—and include collaborations with IEEE Societies—on topics such as AI, machine learning, 5G, and data privacy.

    Get institutional access to the IEEE eLearning Library

    Credentialing Program

    Stay competitive with IEEE credentials. Get up to speed on industry trends and best practices, and earn certificates that accumulate Professional Development Hours (PDHs) and Continuing Education Units (CEUs). After completion, some courses provide digital badges that can be shared on social media.

    Hosting a virtual conference or learning event? IEEE can approve your organization to be a provider of credentials that offer CEUs, PDHs, and Certificates of Completion.

    Accreditation

    Shaping the Future of Professional Education

    Accreditation provides university engineering and engineering technology programs with a credential that assures graduates meet the quality standard for the profession. By participating in the accreditation of engineering and engineering technology programs through ABET, IEEE and its volunteers have a voice in determining the knowledge, skills, and abilities needed for successful entry into engineering, computing, and technology fields.
